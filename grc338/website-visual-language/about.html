About Us

At Lattes and Leaves, we invite you to step into a haven where the rich aroma of freshly brewed lattes mingles with the soothing presence of plants. We are not just a cafe; we are a sanctuary where coffee enthusiasts and plant lovers converge to experience a harmonious blend of flavors and nature.

Born out of a passion for both the robust taste of expertly crafted lattes and the refreshing embrace of greenery, Lattes and Leaves is more than just a coffee shop—it's an oasis. Nestled in the heart of San Luis Obispo, our cafe is a celebration of life's simple pleasures: the warmth of a well-crafted latte and the beauty of thriving plants.

Lattes and Leaves is more than a place to grab a cup of coffee; it's a community hub where connections are forged, ideas are cultivated, and friendships blossom. Join us for events like plant care workshops, latte art sessions, and more, as we foster a space for shared passions.

Whether you're seeking a moment of tranquility amidst the bustle of San Luis Obispo, a cozy spot for a coffee date, or a unique destination to explore plant varieties, Latte and Leaves welcomes you with open arms.
